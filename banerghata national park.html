Bannerghatta National Park

About the Park

Bannerghatta National Park is located in Bangalore, India, and is a popular destination for nature lovers and wildlife enthusiasts. The park is known for its rich biodiversity, including a variety of animals, birds, and plants.

Activities

  • Safari Rides
  • Nature Trails
  • Botanical Garden Visits

Location

The park is situated in the southern part of Bangalore, easily accessible from the city center.

Useful Information

  • Opening Hours: 9 AM - 5 PM
  • Entry Fee: ₹300 for adults, ₹150 for children
