About ISKCON

Iskcon,officially known as the International Society for Krishna Consciousness, is a prominent and serene temple dedicated to Lord Krishna, situated in Hare Krishna Hill, Rajajinagar. Founded in 1997, this sprawling spiritual complex is renowned for its stunning architecture, which combines traditional Indian styles with modern design elements. The temple's central attraction is its grand altar housing beautifully crafted deities of Lord Krishna and Radha, surrounded by intricate artwork and serene surroundings. Beyond its spiritual significance, ISKCON Bangalore is a cultural hub offering various activities including devotional services, educational programs, and cultural events. The temple complex also features a vibrant community dining hall known as "Govinda's," which serves delicious and wholesome vegetarian meals. The peaceful ambiance, coupled with its engaging spiritual and cultural activities, makes ISKCON Bangalore a cherished destination for both devotees and visitors seeking solace and enlightenment.
