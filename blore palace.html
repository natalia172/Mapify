Overview

Bengaluru Palace, built in 1887, is a majestic structure inspired by England’s Windsor Castle. It features stunning Tudor-style architecture, with ornate interiors and expansive grounds, offering a glimpse into the royal heritage of Bengaluru.

Attractions

  • Royal Rooms

  • Beautiful Gardens

Travel Tips

Wear comfortable shoes as the palace grounds are extensive. Avoid visiting during peak hours to enjoy a quieter experience. Check for guided tour options to enhance your visit.
