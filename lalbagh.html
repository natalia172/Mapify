Overview

Lalbagh Botanical Garden, located in Bangalore, is a historic garden established in the 18th century. Known for its vast collection of tropical plants, stunning flower displays, and the iconic Glasshouse, it is a favorite spot for both locals and tourists.

Attractions

  • Glasshouse
  • Flower Show
  • Lalbagh Rock
  • Herb Garden

Gallery

Travel Tips

Wear comfortable walking shoes as the garden is quite large. Bring a hat and sunscreen for sun protection. Check the schedule for flower shows and special events to make the most of your visit.
